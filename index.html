Multifamily Waits for Forbearance
Source: Globe Street
Written By: Erika Morphy

As numerous local and state governments have put in place temporary eviction moratoriums, multifamily owners have received some good news from Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac. Freddie Mac gives borrowers three months of forbearance and the borrowers needs to show distress in the property. Additionally, as part of the the forbearance, owners cannot evict a tenant during that time. As for Fannie Mae, its program requires the borrower to suspend evictions during the forbearance period, plus any longer period required by the CARES act or any state/local law.
